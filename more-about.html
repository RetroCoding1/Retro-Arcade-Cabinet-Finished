More about my retro video game hobby

"What are retro video games? Retro gaming, also known as old school gaming, classic gaming and sometimes vintage gaming is the playing of older generation consoles, computers, handheld games, and arcade games in the present."

Most of my favorite retro video games come from the Nintendo console line, such as the NES (Nintendo Entertainment System), SNES (Super NES), and GBA (Game Boy Advance). As for which games I play, the Mario series, Megaman series, and Intelligent Systems games such as Blazing Blade and Advance Wars are my main go-to games.
