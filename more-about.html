More About my Retro Video Game Hobby

"What are retro video games? Retro gaming, also known as old school gaming, classic gaming and sometimes vintage gaming is the playing of older generation consoles, computers, handheld games, and arcade games in the present."

Most of my favorite retro video games come from the Nintendo console line, such as the NES (Nintendo Entertainment System), SNES (Super NES), and GBA (Game Boy Advance). As for which games I play, the Mario series, Megaman series, Kirby series, and Intelligent Systems games such as Blazing Blade and Advance Wars are my main go-to games. On my home computer I have a whole bunch of emulators and awesome games to play on them. (I'm currently working on Super Mario RPG: Legend of the Seven Stars for the SNES. Give it a try!)

A quick list of the emulators I use include: Mesen (for NES), snes9x (for SNES), and the Visual Boy Advance (for GBA). These are the first emulators I saw for download that had good ratings (though Mesen has some issues). These emulators are equipped with lots of tools including save states which I don't think should be used during gameplay, so I avoid using them where possible. (Though I've made an exception for Ninja Gaiden stage 6 boss deaths. That's just straight up evil.)
